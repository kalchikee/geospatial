About This Demo

Synthetic NDVI derived from land-use baselines and seasonal multipliers for Chicago's 77 community areas across 12 months. Boundaries from the City of Chicago open data portal.

The full pipeline ingests real Sentinel-2 and Landsat 8/9 imagery, processes ~600k Cook County parcels, and stores rasters in PostGIS.
